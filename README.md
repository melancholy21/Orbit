**Orbit** is a next-generation social presence platform designed to bridge the distance between friends. In a world of passive scrolling and disjointed feeds, Orbit brings people back together through **real-time co-presence**, active media streaming, and low-pressure social interactions.

At the core of Orbit is the **Lobby**—a virtual living room where you and your friends can drop in, chat, see who is active, and listen to music together in perfect, real-time sync.

---

## ✨ Features That Set Orbit Apart

### 🎵 Co-Presence & Synced Spotify Rooms (The Lobby)
No more sharing links and clicking play at the same time. Orbit’s proprietary synchronization engine connects directly with Spotify to play, pause, skip, and sync music across everyone's devices simultaneously.
* **Shared Music Queue:** Drop Spotify tracks, reorder the playlist, and build the vibe together.
* **Automatic Device Sync:** Stream on your PC, laptop, or mobile device under a single, unified lobby session.
* **Lurk Mode:** Sit back, mute the music, and read the chat without disrupting the audio stream for the rest of the room.
* **Smart Sleep Timer:** Keeps your battery safe by automatically disconnecting inactive tabs after 30 minutes.

### 💬 Seamless Real-Time Messaging & Feeds
Experience social media the way it was meant to be—instant and alive.
* **Personalized Feed:** Share updates, post reactions, and comment on your friends' posts.
* **Direct Messaging:** Private, instant, secure chat powered by Socket.io, utilizing a mutual-follow friend verification security model.
* **Instant Notifications:** Get real-time alerts for likes, comments, private messages, and gentle "nudges" from friends when they want you to join them online.

### 👤 Expressive Digital Profiles
Your space, your rules.
* **Handles First:** Unified `@username` system for clear identity across suggestion lists, search, and follower models.
* **Interactive Statuses:** Share what you're up to or what you're thinking at a glance.
* **Rich Avatars:** Crop, upload, and update your profile picture on the fly.
